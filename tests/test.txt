Niagara Falls is a massive waterfall located on the Niagara River, on the border of Ontario, Canada, and New York, USA. It is one of the most famous waterfalls in the world and attracts millions of tourists each year. The falls consists of three separate falls: the Horseshoe Falls, the American Falls, and the Bridal Veil Falls. The Horseshoe Falls is the largest and most powerful of the three, with a height of approximately 173 feet and a width of 2,300 feet. The Niagara Falls are a breathtaking natural wonder and a must-visit for anyone visiting the area.
